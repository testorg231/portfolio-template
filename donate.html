CSASPP is entirely run by volunteers. Donor funds are used to pay for disbursements like filing fees, flyers, couriers for service of process, printing, legal scholar consultants, expert witnesses, solicitor fees, web hosting, and other administrative costs. But the bulk of the expenses are allocated towards lawyer fees for drafting and prosecuting our claim. Too much is at stake and so the decision was made early to not cut any corners whenever possible.

Please note that contributors will not enter into a solicitor-client relationship with a lawyer engaged in the litigation. Our lawyer will have no direct duty to account or report on the litigation to you, but we certainly intend to. Our lawyer can only accept instructions from us. If any funds are not used, the law firm will return them to us and we will in turn return them to you pro rata. And lastly, our lawyer will have no involvement with the crowdfunding campaign.
